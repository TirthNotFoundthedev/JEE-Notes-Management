1 Trends: The Fluorine Traps

Bond Dissociation Enthalpy

TRAP ALERT: F-F is weaker than Cl-Cl and Br-Br!

Reason: High lone pair-lone pair repulsion in the small F₂ molecule weakens the bond.

Electron Gain Enthalpy (ΔegH)

Chlorine is MORE negative than Fluorine.

Order: Cl > F > Br > I
(F is too small, incoming electron feels repulsion)

Interhalogens: The "Mutant" Halogens

Compounds like XX', XX'₃, XX'₅, XX'₇. They are more reactive than pure halogens (except F₂) because the X-X' bond is weaker.

Oxoacids Trends

Acidic Strength

HOCl < HClO₂ < HClO₃ < HClO₄

Acidity increases with Oxidation State of Halogen.

Oxidizing Power

HOCl > HClO₂ > HClO₃ > HClO₄

Oxidizing power decreases as stability of ion increases.
